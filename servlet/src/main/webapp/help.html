Help and Support

Welcome to IBS Bank's Help and Support page. Here you will find answers to frequently asked questions (FAQs) and guidance on using our services effectively.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I open an account with ISB Bank?

To open an account, visit any of our branches with your identification documents and speak to our customer service representative.

What are the benefits of using online banking?

Online banking allows you to manage your accounts, transfer funds, and pay bills conveniently from anywhere.

How can I apply for a loan?

You can apply for a loan online through our website or visit a branch to discuss your options with a loan officer.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity on my account?

Contact our customer support immediately to report any suspicious activity on your account.
