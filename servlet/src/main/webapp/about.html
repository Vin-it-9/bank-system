About IBS Bank

IBS Bank, founded on June 29, 2024, by Vinit Shinde, is a leading financial institution based in Pune. Our mission is to provide exceptional banking services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our customers.

With a commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction, IBS Bank offers a wide range of services including personal banking, business solutions, loans, and investment opportunities. We aim to foster long-term relationships built on trust and reliability.

Headquartered in Pune, IBS Bank serves customers nationwide, offering competitive rates, personalized customer service, and innovative financial solutions. Whether you are saving for the future, managing your business finances, or planning your investments, IBS Bank is here to support your financial journey.

At IBS Bank, we value integrity, transparency, and community engagement. We actively contribute to the communities we serve through various initiatives and partnerships aimed at promoting financial literacy and sustainable growth.

Our vision is to be the most trusted financial partner, empowering individuals and businesses to achieve their financial goals with confidence. We continue to innovate and adapt to the evolving needs of our customers, ensuring that we remain at the forefront of the banking industry.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our services and how IBS Bank can help you achieve financial success.
